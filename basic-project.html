Books&Wine Club

For those who love talking about books over a glass of wine

Do you like wine? Do you also like settling down to a good book? Then this club is for you.
It is a place where people can get together and talk about their favorite books over a glass of wine. Books&Wine Club welcomes members to come in their individual book clubs and register as groups for membership. Individuals can also register and be matched with a group of their choice. Being a member of Books&Wine Club gives you access to our picturesque location along Kiambu Road and our extensive wine collection at a discounted price. You will also be able to access our library.
Membership is $20 a month for weekly meetings.
